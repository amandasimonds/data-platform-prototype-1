Welcome to the Data Platform Prototype that I (Amanda Simonds) built!

This prototype was used for usability testing for UIUX design and front end development of a beta release, data platform application.
Our aim was to conduct research on user intuitiveness around the workflows we were designing for.

This prototype demonstrates proposed workflows and features. All visuals and content are placeholders only, and do not represent real-world examples. Feel free to look around!
